## 5. Content Production SOP

### Research Workflow
1. Sign up for free tier/trial (mandatory — never review without testing)
2. Screenshot every key feature and pricing page
3. Test on realistic tasks matching personas
4. Search "[tool] alternatives" and "[tool] vs" for competitive positioning
5. Check G2, Capterra, TrustPilot for real user patterns
6. Check tool's changelog for recent updates
7. Check Reddit for unfiltered opinions
8. Confirm affiliate program status + commission rates
9. Validate keyword with Google autocomplete + PAA

### Review Standards
- Minimum 3 key features with screenshots
- Real output examples, not marketing samples
- Minimum 2 honest "don't buy this if" statements
- Current pricing with plan comparison table
- Minimum 3 pros and 3 cons (genuine)
- Clear verdict with scenario-based guidance
- Minimum 5 screenshots per review
- 2,000-3,500 words (substantive, not padded)

### Comparison Standards
- Same briefs for all tools tested
- Scoring: Features (25%), Output Quality (25%), Pricing Value (20%), Ease of Use (15%), Integrations (15%)
- Winner per scenario, not just overall
- If tools are genuinely equal, say so

### Editorial Style
- Tone: expert friend, not corporate blog
- Short paragraphs (3 lines max)
- Bullet lists, tables, callout boxes liberally
- Break up every 200-300 words with a visual element
- First person "we" voice
- "Last updated: [date]" at top of every article

### AI-Assist Policy
- Use AI for: outlines, brainstorming, drafting tables, summarizing reviews
- Do NOT use AI for: final verdicts, fake testing claims, fabricated output examples
- Every published claim verified by human against actual tool
