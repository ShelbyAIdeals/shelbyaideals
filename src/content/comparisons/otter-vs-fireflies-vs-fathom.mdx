## How We Evaluated

Our evaluation of Otter.ai, Fireflies, and Fathom focused on several key criteria to determine the best AI meeting notes tool. ## Otter.ai: Strengths and Weaknesses

Otter.ai has carved out a strong position in the market as a user-friendly and affordable choice for individuals and small teams. Its **Key Strengths** include:

- **Comprehensive Free Tier**: Offers 600 minutes per month with essential features.
- **Ease of Use**: Intuitive interface suitable for users with minimal technical expertise.
  
However, Otter.ai is not without its **Key Weaknesses**:

- **Limited Integration Options**: Does not integrate as widely as some competitors, which may limit its utility in more complex workflows.
- **Moderate Transcription Accuracy**: While generally reliable, there can be occasional errors in transcription, especially with complex terminology.
