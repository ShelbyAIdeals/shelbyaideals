## What Synthesia Actually Does

Synthesia is specifically an AI avatar video platform. Here is what that means in practice:

1. **AI Avatar Presenters.** Choose from 200+ pre-built avatars or create a custom avatar based on a real person (Enterprise plan). Avatars range from corporate professionals to casual presenters. They look at the camera, gesture naturally, and lip-sync to your script.

2. **Multilingual Video Creation.** Type your script in any of 140+ languages. The avatar delivers it with accurate pronunciation and lip-sync. Create one video, then generate versions in 10 languages in minutes — no re-filming.

3. **Screen Recording Integration.** Combine AI avatar footage with screen recordings, slides, or other visual assets. The avatar appears as a picture-in-picture presenter while you show product demos, dashboards, or slides.

4. **Templates and Branding.** Pre-built video templates for common use cases (training, onboarding, product demos, marketing). Customize with your brand colors, logo, and fonts.

5. **Collaboration.** Multiple team members can create, edit, and approve videos. Built-in commenting and review workflows.

## Who Should Skip Synthesia

### Content Creators Who Need Authentic Personal Brands

If your audience follows you for *you* — your personality, your face, your voice — an AI avatar is not a substitute. YouTubers, influencers, coaches, and thought leaders need to show up on camera themselves. AI avatars can supplement (e.g., for translated versions), but they cannot replace the authentic human connection that personal brands depend on.

### Teams That Need Stock-Footage Videos

If you want to turn blog posts into social videos with b-roll footage, transitions, and captions — that is not what Synthesia does. Synthesia creates avatar-presenter videos. For stock-footage style content, [Pictory](https://pictory.ai?ref=fran26) is purpose-built for that workflow at $19/mo. Read our [guide to whether Pictory is worth it](/guides/is-pictory-worth-it) for details.

### Very Budget-Constrained Teams

At $22/mo for 10 videos, Synthesia is affordable. But if you need 30+ videos per month, the Creator plan at $67/mo is a serious line item. And if quality demands push you toward custom avatars on the Enterprise plan, the cost jumps significantly. Small teams with tight budgets may find that screen-recorded Loom videos accomplish 80% of the same goal at zero cost.

### Teams Needing Cinematic Quality

Synthesia avatars look professional and convincing, but they are not indistinguishable from real humans in every scenario. Close-up, full-screen avatar shots sometimes reveal the subtle uncanny-valley tells — slightly unnatural eye movement, repetitive gestures. For high-production brand videos destined for TV or prime placement, real actors still win.

## Tips for Marketing Teams Using Synthesia

1. **Script quality is everything.** The avatar delivers exactly what you write. Invest time in writing natural, conversational scripts. Read them aloud before rendering — if they sound awkward spoken, they will sound awkward from the avatar too.

2. **Combine avatar footage with screen recordings.** Pure talking-head avatar videos get monotonous after 2-3 minutes. Break up the avatar shots with product demos, slides, charts, or animations. Synthesia's built-in screen recording integration makes this easy.

3. **Test multiple avatars with your brand.** Some avatars feel more natural than others for specific content types. Corporate training might suit a professional avatar, while a product announcement might work better with a casual, energetic one. Test before committing to a brand standard.

4. **Use the multilingual feature strategically.** If you have an international audience, creating one video and rendering it in 5 languages multiplies your content's reach with minimal additional effort. This is one of Synthesia's highest-ROI features.

5. **Batch your production.** Render all your videos for the week in one session. This is more efficient than creating one-offs and helps you stay within your monthly video credit limit.
