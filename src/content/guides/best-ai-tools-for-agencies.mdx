Running an agency means delivering client work at scale while keeping margins healthy. Every hour your team spends on repetitive production work — writing first drafts, editing video, building reports, formatting content — is an hour not spent on strategy, client relationships, and the creative work that actually grows your business.

AI tools change that equation fundamentally. Not by replacing your team, but by compressing the production phase of every deliverable. A blog post that took 4 hours to research, draft, and optimize now takes 90 minutes. A client video that required a production crew and a week of turnaround now takes an afternoon. A monthly report that someone manually assembled from five dashboards now builds itself.

This guide covers the AI tools that deliver the highest ROI for agencies — digital marketing agencies, content agencies, SEO agencies, and creative agencies. We include specific pricing, how the tools work together, and two stack recommendations: one for agencies just starting with AI and one for teams ready to go all-in.

---

## The Agency AI Challenge

Agencies have different needs than solo creators or in-house teams:

- **Multiple clients with different brand voices.** You cannot use a one-size-fits-all AI writing template. Tools need to adapt per client.
- **Volume demands.** Agencies produce more content, more videos, more reports than individual businesses. Per-unit pricing matters.
- **Quality standards.** You are selling your output to paying clients. "Good enough for a blog post" is not good enough for a client deliverable.
- **Workflow integration.** Your team uses project management, communication, and delivery tools. AI tools need to plug into existing workflows, not create parallel ones.
- **Margin protection.** Every tool subscription hits your overhead. The tool must save more in labor costs than it adds in subscription costs.

With those constraints in mind, here are the eight tools that earn their place in an agency stack.

## 1. Jasper — AI Writing with Client Brand Voices ($49/mo)

**What it does:** [Jasper](/reviews/jasper-ai-review) generates marketing content — blog posts, ad copy, social captions, landing pages, email sequences, product descriptions — using AI trained on your client's brand voice, tone, and style guidelines.

**Why agencies need it:** Jasper's brand voice feature is the differentiator for agencies. You set up a brand profile for each client — their tone (professional, casual, witty), their terminology, their audience — and Jasper adjusts its output accordingly. Write for Client A in a formal B2B voice, then switch to Client B's playful D2C voice without changing tools.

**Pricing for agencies:** The Creator plan at $49/mo gives one brand voice and SEO mode. The Business plan (custom pricing, starts around $125/mo) supports multiple brand voices, which is what agencies actually need. For 5+ clients with distinct brands, the Business plan is the practical choice.

**Time saved:** 1-2 hours per blog post draft, 30 minutes per set of ad variations, 15 minutes per social media batch. For an agency producing 20 pieces of content per week, that is 20-40 hours of production time reclaimed monthly.

**Alternative for budget-conscious agencies:** [Copy.ai](/reviews/copy-ai-review) at $36/mo offers similar content generation capabilities with slightly less sophisticated brand voice features. If brand consistency across multiple clients is less critical (or managed by editors), Copy.ai is the more affordable choice.

## 2. Surfer SEO — Content Optimization at Scale ($89/mo)

**What it does:** [Surfer SEO](/reviews/surfer-seo-review) analyzes top-ranking pages for any keyword and provides real-time optimization scores as your team writes. It tells writers exactly what terms to include, what heading structure to use, and how comprehensive the article needs to be to compete.

**Why agencies need it:** SEO content agencies live and die by rankings. Surfer removes the guesswork by showing writers exactly what Google rewards for each keyword. The result: more consistent quality across different writers, higher first-page ranking rates, and data-backed proof of optimization quality for client reporting.

**Pricing for agencies:** Essential at $89/mo (30 articles) works for small agencies. Scale at $129/mo (100 articles) is the sweet spot for agencies publishing 15-25 articles per week across clients. The Google Docs and WordPress plugins let writers use Surfer without changing their workflow.

**Client deliverable bonus:** Export Surfer's content score as proof of optimization quality in client reports. "Your article scored 87/100 against top-ranking competitors" is a tangible metric clients appreciate.

**Budget alternative:** [Frase](https://www.frase.io/?via=shelby-ai) at $15-45/mo provides 80% of Surfer's optimization capability with built-in AI writing. For agencies where content optimization is important but not the core service, Frase offers better value. See our [Frase buying guide](/guides/is-frase-worth-it).

## 3. Pictory — Blog-to-Video at Scale ($19/mo)

**What it does:** [Pictory](https://pictory.ai?ref=fran26) converts written content into polished videos with stock footage, captions, transitions, and music. Paste a blog post, get a video.

**Why agencies need it:** Video is the most-requested deliverable that agencies struggle to scale. Traditional video production requires a crew, equipment, and editing time. Pictory lets your content team produce video versions of every blog post and article without a dedicated video producer.

**Pricing for agencies:** Starter at $19/mo for 30 videos. Professional at $39/mo for 60 videos with priority rendering and API access (useful for automation). Teams at $99/mo for unlimited videos and collaboration features.

**Agency workflow:** Writer publishes blog post. Pictory turns it into a 60-second social video and a 3-minute YouTube version. Your team reviews and brand-customizes. Client gets blog post plus two video assets from one piece of content — tripling the deliverable value with minimal additional effort.

## 6. Frase — Research and Writing for Content Teams ($15/mo)

**What it does:** [Frase](https://www.frase.io/?via=shelby-ai) combines content research, AI writing, and SEO optimization. Enter a keyword, get a research brief from top-ranking pages, write with AI assistance, and optimize in real time.

**Why agencies need it:** For agencies where content writers handle both research and drafting, Frase consolidates the workflow into one tool. The research briefs save 30-60 minutes per article by automating competitor analysis. The AI writer helps junior writers produce better first drafts. The optimization score ensures consistent SEO quality.

**Pricing for agencies:** Solo at $15/mo for 10 documents (good for freelance agencies). Basic at $45/mo for 30 documents (suitable for most small agencies). Team at $115/mo for unlimited documents.

**When to choose Frase over Surfer:** If your agency needs both content generation and optimization in one tool, and budget is a factor. If SEO optimization depth is the priority and you have a separate writing workflow, Surfer is worth the premium.

## How the Tools Work Together (Agency Workflow)

Here is a real workflow for producing a client content package: blog post + social video + voiceover video.

**Step 1: Keyword research with Mangools (15 min).** Find target keywords for the client's next content batch. Analyze competition and search volume.

**Step 2: Content brief with Frase or Surfer (10 min).** Create optimization briefs for each keyword. Share with writers.

**Step 3: Draft with Jasper or Copy.ai (45 min).** Writer generates a first draft using the client's brand voice profile. Reviews and edits for accuracy and voice.

**Step 4: Optimize with Surfer or Frase (15 min).** Polish the article against the optimization score. Ensure comprehensive topic coverage.

**Step 5: Video with Pictory (20 min).** Paste the published article into Pictory. Generate a 60-second social clip and a 3-minute explainer video.

**Step 6: Voiceover with ElevenLabs (5 min).** Generate professional narration for the video. Import into Pictory or video editor.

**Step 7: Avatar video with Synthesia (15 min).** For clients that need presenter-led content, create an avatar version of the key messages.

**Step 8: Distribute with Make.com (0 min — automated).** Publish blog post, schedule social videos, send client notification, update project tracker. All automated.

**Total active time: approximately 2 hours for a complete content package** that includes a blog post, two video formats, and professional voiceover. Traditional production for the same package: 8-12 hours minimum.
