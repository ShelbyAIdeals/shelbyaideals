## Video Production Workflow

### Equipment Needed
- **Screen recording**: OBS Studio (free) or Loom
- **Voiceover**: Your mic or ElevenLabs AI voice
- **Editing**: Descript (eat your own dog food!) or CapCut
- **Thumbnails**: Canva Pro

### Video Structure Template (2-3 min per tool)
1. **Hook** (10s): "Here's what [Tool] can do that no other AI tool can..."
2. **Quick overview** (20s): What it is, who it's for
3. **Live demo** (60-90s): Show the unique features
4. **Pricing** (15s): Quick plan overview
5. **Verdict** (15s): Who should use it + CTA to full review

### Batch Production Schedule
- **Week 1**: Record Jasper + Copy.ai walkthroughs
- **Week 2**: Record Writesonic + Surfer SEO walkthroughs
